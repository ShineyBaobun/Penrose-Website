Our Upcomming Issue

We are now taking submissions for the 4th Issue!

For the fourth issue we have decided to introduce the theme : Everyday STEM

We are welcoming any original ideas relating to the STEM behind everyday experiences, but if you would like to write an article following this theme but are unsure what you would like to write about,  some examples may include :

  • Why Spicy Food Feels Hot
  • Why You Can’t Microwave a Metal Spoon
  • How Your Wi-Fi Decides Who Gets the Fastest Internet
  • The Science of Earthquake-Resistant Architecture
  • The Chemistry of Crying
  • Positive Aura: You Are Technically Glowing - Just Really Dimly
  • The Technology Behind Clean Water
  • The Strength Behind Cardboard
  • Behind the Scenes of Your Morning Coffee: The Chemistry of Brewing
  • Why Your Toast Always Lands Butter-Side Down
  • Your Face is Data: How Facial Recognition Sees You
  • Why You Crave Sugar: A Biological Perspective
  • How Your Body Talks to Itself Using Chemicals

If you would like to submit an article outisde this theme, we will be accepting that as well.

Our deadline for submissions is the 6th of July
